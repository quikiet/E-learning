LearnSmart Policy and Enforcement Guidelines

At LearnSmart, we strive to maintain a safe and respectful community for all students and instructors.

Instructors are prohibited from posting lesson content that violates community guidelines or includes sensitive material. Any breach of this rule will initiate our enforcement process.

The escalation process for policy violations is designed to address non-compliance effectively:

  1. First Violation (Warning) → Instructors receive an email notification from Trust & Safety detailing the violation and relevant policies.
  2. Second Violation (Strike 1) → If the same prohibited content is posted again after a warning, the offending course will be immediately locked, and the instructor’s account will be reviewed.
  3. Third Violation (Strike 2) → Permanent ban of the instructor’s account and all associated course materials from the LearnSmart platform.

Violations are tracked at the course level, affecting all instructors and co-instructors associated with the course in question. We reserve the right to escalate penalties based on the severity of the violation or the instructor’s history.

Revenue sharing for instructors is as follows: Instructors receive 37% of course revenue if learners do not use a coupon. If a coupon is applied, instructors earn 97% of the revenue. We encourage instructors to promote their courses with their own coupons on external platforms to maximize earnings. Revenue will be disbursed monthly to the instructor’s PayPal email address.
